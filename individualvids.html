Gas and stars, side by side

These movies place each of the MW-mass galaxies in FIRE-2 (as of roughly summer 2017) alongside one another. The galaxies are ordered by decreasing "diskiness" (defined in Garrison-Kimmel et al., in prep) at redshift zero. The gas is on the left, with the colors corresponding to the temperature of the gas, and pseudo-color images of the stars are shown on the right.
